Kapyong Barracks has been sold to Indigenous people, they are going to start to make a shopping area and more. This area will be called Naawi Oodena which is Anishinaabe and in English means centre of the heart and community. The land is 160 acres and they have this land do to land agreements set in Treaty 1. Indigenous people were short on the land they where supposed to get, so if there was crown land that they were going to sell, the Indigenous people would get to say it they wanted to buy it or not first. At first, the goverment was going to sell it to a company, that would've disregarded Treaty 1.

Naawi Oodena is going to cost 1.2 billion dollar.the seven treaty one First nations and Canada Lands Company CLC are going to work to gether to make Naawi Oodena. The plan for Naawi Oodena is to have green space, recreation facilities, housing,retal and commercial options. When Naawi Oodena is all done they are expecting to be abell to hold 2,300 and 3,00 homes.
